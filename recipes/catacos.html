Carne Asada Taco Recipe

The smell will tell the story better than the picture, For sure!

Prep Your Carne

The first step to creating this master piece is to get your steak ready to cook!Usualy the steak comes prep-packaged and ground up for you, so all you have to do is add the steak to a skillet and start cooking! Beef makes its own oil so we just need seasoning. I use my favorite "O Del Paso" tacoe seasoning as well as a little garlic, and ground black pepper. The key here is to pay attention to the steak, you dont want it to over-cook or under-cook some pieces. Cookins the steak usually takes around 10-20 Miutes, no more than 25 though.

Corn Or Flour?

Here is where you get your tortillas ready! I use corn, all you have to do is add a little oil to your skillet, and let the tortillas cook for about 3-5 Minutes. They're ready to add Carne!

Toppings?

Mmmmm, The topppings! The best Ones I add are onions and cilantro, I also add salsa verde. Once you add everything that you wanted, You're finished! Enjoy your creation!

All Ingredients

  • Ground Steak
  • Garlic Powder
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Corn/Flour Tortillas
  • Chopped Onions
  • Chopped Cilantro
  • Salsa Verde(your choice brand)
